Day 1: ARRIVAL AND GANGA AARTI

Time Activity
Morning Arrive in Varanasi and check into your hotel (usually around 12:00 PM).
Evening Enjoy the Ganga Aarti ceremony at Dashashwamedh Ghat.
Night Overnight stay at your hotel.

Day 2: CULTURAL IMMERSION

Read More Book Now
Time Activity
Morning Witness Subah-e-Banaras at Assi Ghat and take a boat ride on the Ganges and visit Kashi Vishwanath Temple.
AfterNoon City tour includes Bharat Mata Temple, Sankat Mochan , Durga Temple, Tulsi Manas Temple, New Vishwanath Temple ( Birla Temple).
Evening Stroll through the vibrant markets of Varanasi, known for silk sarees and handicrafts.
Night Overnight stay at your hotel.
